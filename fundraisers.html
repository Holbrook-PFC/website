PFC Fundraisers

Our fundraising efforts will help support various school needs, including sponsoring educational computer programs and apps, classroom projects, school events, in-class biligual aides and the purchase of supplies for our library, music and STEM programs.

We are always looking for volunteers. To learn more about how to volunteer at the school during events, please visit the MDUSD website.

Walk-A-Thon

Each year, our students lace up their running shoes and hit the track to help raise funds for school projects and programs. It’s a fun and active way for students to get involved and make a difference in their school community!

Dollar-A-Day

We ask families to consider donating $365 per student for the year — that's just $1 a day to help support vital programs, classroom supplies, events, and enrichment activities throughout the school year.

Every dollar makes a difference, and your contribution helps create an exceptional experience for all our students!

Book Fairs

The PFC proudly hosts two book fairs each year: Fall Book Fair (October): Held during the week of Fall Fest, featuring books from Paper Pie (formerly known as Usborne Books). Spring Book Fair (April): Held during the week of Spring Fest, in partnership with Scholastic Books.

These events are a great opportunity to support literacy and help raise funds for our school community
